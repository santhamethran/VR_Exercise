
“This is the Fingertip Touch Exercise.
"This helps to maintain joint motion and decrease joint stiffness.”

“In this exercise the left hand will be holding the fingers straight and close together.”

“Forming a circle by touching thumb to each fingertip.”
 
“It will be moving slowly and smoothly, touching the index finger. Follow with the middle, ring, and small fingers.”

“You may begin the exercise when ready.”
