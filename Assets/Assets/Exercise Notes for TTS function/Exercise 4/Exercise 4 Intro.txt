“This exercise called the The Knuckle Bend.  
"This exercise helps maintain joint motion and decrease joint stiffness as well.”

“It will start by holding the hand and fingers straight out and close together.” 

“It will Bend the middle joints of the fingers.” 

“Keeping the knuckles straight.”

“While moving slowly and smoothly.”

“Afterwards the hand will return to the starting position.”

“Please continue to the exercise.”
